- The control plane is the management side of the cluster.
- In these notes, the main control plane components are [[04 kube-api]], [[06 Controller-Manager]], [[07 kube-scheduler]], and the state store [[02 ETCD]].
- In kubeadm-based clusters, these components are commonly managed as [[10 Static Pods]].
